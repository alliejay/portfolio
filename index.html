ABOUT ALISON

Albert Schweitzer's quote regarding success and happiness embodies the ideals which I believe whole-heartedly, and which have changed my life for the better in this past year.

I graduated from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst in 2012 and shortly thereafter started a career in the Cell Tower Industry. While I enjoyed my role as a Lease Analyst, and I had the utmost respect for my managers & co-workers, I (like most) am not overwhelmingly interested in steel structures. I found myself envying those who went to work every day truly loving what they did.

I moved from Boston, MA (where I had lived my entire life) to Charleston, SC in March of 2015 and shortly after stopped working at the cell tower company. It was the perfect time to change career paths and choose a field that I have an organic interest in. It quickly became apparent that web development & design brings out a side of me I hadn't seen in years. I get excited to go to school every day and learn more about the amazing things I will become capable of as a front end engineer.

I am currently enrolled at The Iron Yard for their Fall 2015 Cohort - Front End Engineering, and will be graduating on January 8th. I'll routintely update with projects I've worked on, learning moments, and other personal infortmation. If you wish to contact me for employment opportunities or just to chat, please visit the contact page.
